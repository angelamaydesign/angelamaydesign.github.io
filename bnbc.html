Booze & Book Club

Booze + Book Club meets monthly to discuss the novel whilst drinking novel based cocktails.

Goal:

Create a book club brand that uses booze reading as a discussion.

Insight:

The original book club logo was built to represent booze and books in a modernized religious manner. I wanted to play up that in more of an old book related way. I decided to change the font to an old book sans serif, and replicate it as an old printing press.

Outcome:

Strong community of over 200 avid readers.
